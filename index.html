Hello, I'm a Designer & Developer

my name is Francis, some people call me Frank



Born in Lomé, West Africa.   Raised in Moscow, Russia.   Living in New York City, USA.   Yea, I know... Diplomat parents.   My undergratuate was a Bachelor's in Business Administration with a concentration on Hospitality Managment, which lead to a 10 year career in Beverage Operations Management (alcohol, yes), and Nightlife Managment - "Boooorrrriiing!" - I want to build things.   So, creativity and visualization of concepts, along with problem-solving have always come naturally to me.   Design by principle, however, is a skill I have worked very hard to gain a high level in, along with software development and architecture.   The front-end was the apple of my eye.   Then, somewhere between 2017 - 2018, I completed 2 highly acclaimed and rigourous Software Development Intensives (and countless other certifications), as well as a Data Science intensive where I was able to put industry and professional best-practices to the test on various projects.   Over the last four years, I have freelanced, interned, and worked at various companies, start-ups, and on one-off projects.   I currently work as a Data Engineer at Cognizant.   I speak fluent Javascript, Ruby, Python, PHP, and Java.   I also can pick up whatever language you may require in a relatively short time.   In my spare time :D, I run a design studio and a digital agency of my own, where I am the principal "everything".   I've done work for past clients in the likes of Huge, Inc, Grey Goose, Moēt & Hennessy, and Puma.   Thanks for listening, checkout some projects below, and please do get in touch.
